2019 Chevrolet Corvette Stingray

more pictures here

$00,00

This is a gorgeous example of the Viper coupe with the rare Race Yellow exterior with black stripes and black interior. This car originally came from the US and had been in a significant accident but has been repaired and restored to its current like-new condition. There was no frame damage nor air bag deployment but the right side panels were, apparently, extensively damaged. That’s the bad news.

The good news is the repair was done thoroughly and correctly and is now back to original condition. I inspected the car in detail before purchasing it and found it to be in terrific shape with no cover ups or telltale signs. The further good news is that because the car has been in an accident, even though it is superbly repaired so you would never know it now, it is priced thousands less than a comparable Viper. All the mechanicals are in top shape: brakes, suspension, transmission, drive train, electrics, etc. The car has only 44,000 miles (70 kms) since new.

It has an after market sport exhaust system which gives it a really throaty growl which matches the cars aggressive looks.

I would be more than happy for you (or someone you choose) to inspect the car thoroughly as it is great condition and is a great value. I’m confident about the car; I’m not as confident in your ability to keep it within the speed limits and avoid a ticket.
