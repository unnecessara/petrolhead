No matter where you live , this i8 will be the coolest car on your block. It has gorgeous super car looks and the butterfly doors are worth the price of admission alone. But this car isn’t just another pretty face......it is loaded with technology, starting with the 1.5 L turbocharged 3 cylinder gas engine paired with the electric power combines to develop a whopping 357 HP and 420 pounds of torque while consuming about the same amount of gas as your neighbors Toyota Prius.

All of this translates into a 0-100 km/hr of only 4.3 seconds. Seriously quick and seriously fun to drive. AWD and sophisticated suspension keep this car pointed in the direction you pointed it even if you were a bit over zealous....OK, downright silly.

Original sticker price was well north of $150,000 and although it still looks and drives like new , you can now own it for about half of that.

Under 60000 original kms!

This car came with many options including 360 degree cameras, navigation, Head Up Display, park distance control, LED lights, Tire Pressure monitoring plus tons more..

Accident free CARFAX which is not that easy to find.

The exterior is finished in stunning Protonic Blue with complimentary blue interior lighting , black leather with blue stitching and even the optional blue seat belts.

In short, this is a space age looking super car that is fun fun yet practical to own. A blast to drive and even fun just to sit and stare at it.
