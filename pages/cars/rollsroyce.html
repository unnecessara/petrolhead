1965 Rolls Royce Silver Cloud III

Load more pictures

Sold!

If ever there was an automobile that epitomizes the classic British luxury vehicle, it is the Rolls Royce Silver Cloud III. It commands attention and is majestic just sitting there.

Built by Rolls Royce between 1963 and 1966, the Silver Cloud III is still recognized as one of the most beautiful luxury sedans ever built. Originally finished in silver, a recent detailed restoration now presents the car in Rolls Royce period correct two tone Champagne over Burgundy. The red Connelly hides have been painstakingly restored to their original splendour with a hint of appropriate patina befitting their age.

The woodwork is simply stunning and looks in new condition. The 6.2 1itre Rolls Royce V8 makes this automobile a joy to drive. Many service records are available and include just completed SU carb rebuilds, two new master brake cylinders and new seals in the steering box, new filters, and complete servicing.

New wide white wall BG Goodrich Silvertown Radials have just been installed. Original tool kit, including flashlight, and original owners manual are included.
